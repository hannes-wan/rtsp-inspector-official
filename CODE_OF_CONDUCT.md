# Community conduct

The RTSP Inspector issue tracker is a technical workspace. Be specific, patient, and respectful; critique behavior and evidence rather than people. Harassment, threats, discrimination, doxxing, spam, and publication of private customer material are not accepted.

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove material that violates this standard and may restrict repeated abuse. Report sensitive conduct concerns privately to [support@hannes-software.com](mailto:support@hannes-software.com) instead of escalating them in a public thread.
